Mongolia

Bayangiin Nuruu Petroglyphs

Area with many prehistoric cliff engravings featuring people and various animials. Many locals consider that some of the drawings show aliens in helmets.

Bayanzag (The Flaming Cliffs)

One of the richest and most interesting fossil finds on Earth. This was the first site where dinosaur eggs were found. Velociraptor and other Cretaceous dinosaurs have also been uncovered here.

Amarbayasgalant Monastery

One of most interesting monasteries in Mongolia, constructed between 1727 – 1736. 28 temples currently remain on a 207 x 175 m large platform.
